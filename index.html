About Me

Hi. I’m Scott Currie, a full stack web developer in the Greater Seattle Area, specializing in HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript, and Python. Prior to web development, I spent nearly 15 years in IT and held positions of increasing complexity and responsibility, most recently as a Linux/Windows systems administrator.

Throughout my career, I’ve been motivated by a fascination with technology and a drive to solve complex problems. As I honed my skills in systems administration, I recognized that the most engaging and satisfying problems I faced were solved through writing code. In fact, creating novel solutions to problems through code is so rewarding that it often doesn’t feel like work.

As I set about pursuing the passion to solve big problems through code, I connected with new passions: a desire to lead; openness to diversity and inclusivity; a desire to solve problems that are not just personally rewarding, but important. As my career develops, I envision myself working in diverse teams as both collaborator and mentor, writing the best code I can in service of improving society.
